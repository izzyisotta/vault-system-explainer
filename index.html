The Vault Restructure Plan defines a build ladder. Stages 1, 2 and 4a are in decent shape; Stage 0 (lock the schema) has not happened, and everything downstream of it is waiting. The plan's own words: "Critical path: 0 → 1 → 2 → 3."

Metadata: current vs proposed

From Vault Metadata - Current vs Proposed.xlsx and the canon Vault Restructure Plan.xlsx (which wins all disagreements). The header on the sheet says it all: "PROPOSED (Freddie-adapted) — capture stays dumb, vault holds all metadata."

The rule of thumb (verbatim from the plan): "Does this thing have (or deserve) its own note? YES → link it (entity). NO, it's a value describing the note → field (attribute). Only loose keywords → tag."
FieldChangeWhat it becomes
title date_created date_modified type status source page_roleKeepUnchanged universal fields
tagsDemotedPerson-name tags, voice-transcript, genuine keywords only. The controlled axes move out to their own fields; tags stop being the dumping ground
areasNewLife-domain axis, vault-wide, multi-value: the to-do system's area vocabulary promoted to every note. Primary dashboard facet
effortsNewBounded pushes that cut across areas: Signal Internship, SSUP, AI Club, AI Workflow, INSEAD
peopleNewWikilinks to People notes: the entity axis that powers the CRM and Related queries
relatedNewWikilinks to companies / projects / concepts for graph + backlinks
descriptionNewOne-liner for dashboards, distinct from the body Summary
interests themesKeepIntellectual axis (4 values) and creative axis (11, creative-only), unchanged
content_formatExtendedAdds meeting, talk, call, workshop, 1-1, interview: absorbs the "category" idea
audio enriched_data recorder duration_minsNew (voice)Voice-origin fields, matching Dad's pipeline assets
actionsOptionalOpen follow-ups per note; the future feed into the Task System
The one big exception: INSEAD stays folder-structured (subject/lecture fields + Sources/L##). The flat scheme is the go-forward default for everything non-INSEAD, with no mass migration: the ~875-file back catalogue gets an additive backfill (folder → areas/efforts/Up:), and evolves opportunistically.
